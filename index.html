Chicken biryani

Chicken Biryani is a traditional South Asian dish made with rice, spices, and chicken. It is usually seasoned with aromatic spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, bay leaves, cumin, coriander, and others, layered with marinated chicken and cooked until the rice is fully cooked and the flavors have melded together.
